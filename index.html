WORKFLOW02

Before an idea becomes reality, it has to make sense. I try to think about customer requirements and user needs first, the two essential ingredients for a successful project.

First Contact
At a first meeting, I like setting out the objectives and scope of the project, as well as the possible deadlines and budget. I also usually explain an example of how I do my job, if necessary I schedule additional meetings.

Planning
Once the scope of the project has been approved, I draw up a possible roadmap. First, I bring all the ideas to light and put them in writing. Second, defining the possible technologies to be used. And finally, taking into account the possible problems and their solutions before starting the project.

Design, Prototype & Build
To create a good design, I need to understand its content and create a value around it. After understanding this, I start the creation process, where I create possible designs that I review with the client. When the client gives the approval, I start with the construction but without forgetting the client, giving him the opportunity to continue contributing.

Handover, Launch and Ongoing support
Once the project is completed, all files will be delivered for operation, and if necessary I will write any help documentation. In addition the client can always contact me in case any problems arise or need help.
